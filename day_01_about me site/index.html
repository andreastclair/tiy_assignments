Hello there! I'm Andrea St. Clair.

I'm a doodler, dancer, and 80s music enthusiast living in Austin, Texas. I blew into town from Oklahoma seven years ago, drawn to all of the vibrant colors that Austin has to offer. What's my favorite color, you ask? I love all of the colors! So I found my happy place in a city that is rich with culture, music, food, and art. Since moving here, I've been lucky enough to dance my socks off to a few 80s favs including Hall and Oates, New Order, The Cure, Cyndi Lauper, Blondie, and Depeche Mode. And almost nightly, Bowie makes a speacial appearance in my living room.

I live with my husband of almost one year and together, we have two cats, an excellently eclectic vinyl collection, and an affinity for action movies, pizza, and bourbon.

Meet my quirky little family.
