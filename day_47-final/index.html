OUR PARTNERS


We have been fortunate enough to partner with some of Austin's largest organizations in the dance community. With their support, we have been able to hold events benefiting our young dancers and have gained our own footing in the community. If you're interested in becoming a partner, we would love to hear from you.

DONATE


Our site generates donations from costume sales. Of each costume sold, 10% of the proceeds are donated directly to dance programs for underprivileged children. To find out more about becoming a seller, read our Seller's Handbook. Don't have any costumes to sell? You can still donate to our little dancers here. Thank you for your kindness and support.
